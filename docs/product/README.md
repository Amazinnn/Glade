# Product Thinking Library

This folder records valuable product conversations, design principles, and decisions for AI Life Assistant.

The purpose is not to create heavy documentation. It is to preserve the thinking that should guide product and engineering choices as the project evolves.

## Files

- `philosophy.md`: stable product philosophy, principles, and anti-goals.
- `conversation-log.md`: dated notes from product conversations.
- `decisions.md`: explicit product decisions and their rationale.

## Working Agreement

- Capture ideas that may influence product direction, interaction design, privacy, data model, or user experience.
- Separate stable principles from exploratory conversation.
- Prefer concise notes over transcripts.
- When a conversation leads to a clear decision, add it to `decisions.md`.
- Keep the product's philosophy connected to implementation choices.
